Informed Consent

You are about to participate in a research study on cognitive fatigue detection using passive keyboard and mouse signals.

What will be captured

  • Keystroke timings: when keys are pressed and released (timestamps only)
  • Key codes: which key was pressed (letter keys are recorded; we do NOT save the words you type)
  • Mouse coordinates: cursor position sampled every 100ms
  • Click events: when and where you click
  • Prakriti quiz answers: your A/B/C responses

What will NOT be captured

  • Your typed words or text content (only individual keystroke timings)
  • Personal identifying information
  • Screen contents, screenshots, or video
  • Any data outside this browser tab

Your rights

  • You may stop the session at any time by closing the browser tab.
  • You may request your data be deleted by contacting the research team.
  • All data will be exported as a CSV file at the end of the session for your reference.
